About Us

Who are we

GreenBus is the number 1 inter-city bus ticketing service in the world. GreenBus is a part of Greenbus , India's primary online travel group that also owns the leading hotels and air aggregator, Greenbus


Our Credentials

Founded in 2006 in Bangalore, India, GreenBus now connects various cities across the globe with

just a click of a button.GreenBus has revolutionized the online bus ticket booking industry by bringing together over 2,000 bus operators covering over 100,000 routes across countries.

With its mobile app downloaded by over 7.5 million loyal customers, GreenBus is the largest community of bus travellers in the world


So Why Book with Us?

GreenBus provides bus travellers, the most uncomplicated and hassle-free booking experience ever.

Choose your destination, select bus based on user ratings, view the seat layout, choose convenient seats, and book your ticket in just a few clicks!

The GreenBus experience does not end with the booking.

In a series of industry leading features, GreenBus offers navigation to bus boarding points, real-time tracking of buses and information on rest stops.

GreenBus also partners with leading bus operators to provide exclusive "Red Deals" to customers booking on GreenBus.

A dedicated 24*7 customer-care team is available to cater to all the passengers needs while they are on-the- go.
